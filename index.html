About Me


Hello, i am a 23 years old computer science student, with a talent and a passion for drawing. I have been drawing and painting since early childhood. Before I started studying computer science, I was a member of the studio "Atelje Az" (https://ateljeaz.blogspot.com/) for many years, and I competed in various art competitions.
